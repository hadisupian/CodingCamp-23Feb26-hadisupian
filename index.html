About The Company

Nusa Inovasi Digital (NID) is a digital transformation catalyst based in Jakarta. Starting from a small garage with three visionaries in 2018, we have now grown into a strategic partner for over 200 companies in adopting cutting-edge technology. We are not just an IT agency; we are solution architects who believe that technology should work for people, not the other way around. With a team consisting of coding experts, creative designers, and data analysts, we help traditional businesses leap into the digital era with strategies that are measurable, efficient, and of course, sustainable.

Our Vision

To become the global gold standard in providing inclusive, smart digital ecosystem solutions capable of empowering business scales to achieve their full potential in the future.

Our Mission

To accelerate industrial transformation by providing secure digital infrastructure, developing solution-oriented AI-driven software, and empowering partners through technical education while ensuring the highest standards of data integrity and code quality in every project.

My Projects

Smart Retail Ecosystem (SRE)

A comprehensive digital transformation for a national retail network with over 50 branches. This project includes the development of a real-time inventory system integrated with Point of Sale (POS) applications and a cloud-based analytics dashboard. With this implementation, the client successfully reduced stock discrepancies by up to 15% and significantly accelerated the monthly financial reporting process.

EduGate: Learning Management System

A custom online learning platform designed specifically for professional certification training institutions. The primary focus of this project was on intuitive user experience (UX) and server scalability to accommodate thousands of students simultaneously. Key features include an automated examination system, student learning progress monitoring, and secure digital payment integration.

GreenTech Infrastructure Migration

An IT infrastructure migration project from on-premise servers to a hybrid-cloud environment for a renewable energy company. We handled the entire large database relocation process with zero downtime. This project not only enhanced company data security but also optimized server operational costs by up to 30% per year through efficient virtualization techniques.
